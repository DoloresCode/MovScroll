MovScroll

MovScroll is a dynamic and interactive database of movies, designed to cater to film enthusiasts and casual viewers alike. Here, you can find detailed information on a wide array of movies, including their titles, release dates, runtimes, genres, and more. This project was created with the aim of making it easier to discover and explore the fascinating world of cinema.

Use Cases

MovScroll serves as a go-to platform for movie lovers seeking to explore new films, revisit classics, or simply learn more about their favorite flicks. It's also a valuable tool for those interested in the film production process, providing detailed information about actors and more.
